Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01-02-2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using Image Resizer. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or images processed through the extension. All image processing is performed locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data usage beyond providing the intended resizing functionality.

4. Data Security

All image processing is conducted locally within the browser. No images or data are sent to external servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising services.

6. Compliance with Chrome Web Store Policies

We strictly adhere to Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies, ensuring that the extension serves a single purpose—image resizing—without unnecessary permissions or data collection.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the extension after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
